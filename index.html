Guitar Dashboard

An interactive music theory dashboard, including a circle of fifths for guitarists. By Mike Hadlow.

  • Select a mode by clicking on a mode button.
  • Select the tonic by clicking on a note in the circle. The current tonic is highlighted in yellow.
  • Click on the outer 'chord' ring to show the triad associated with that note. Click on a note to cancel the chord display.
  • Chord ring colours:
    • Green: major chord.
    • Blue: minor chord.
    • Red: diminished chord.

Open source on Github.
