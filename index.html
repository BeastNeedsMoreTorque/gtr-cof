Guitar Dashboard
Info

An interactive music theory dashboard, including a circle of fifths for guitarists.

  • Select the tonic by clicking on a note button. The current tonic is highlighted in yellow.
  • Select a mode by clicking on a mode button.
  • Click on the outer 'chord' ring of either the chromatic note circle or the circle of fifths to show the triad associated with that note. Click on a note to cancel the chord display.
  • Chord ring colours:
    • Green: major chord.
    • Blue: minor chord.
    • Red: diminished chord.

By Mike Hadlow. Open source on Github.
