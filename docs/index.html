Guitar Dashboard
Info

An interactive music theory dashboard, including a circle of fifths for guitarists.

Follow @mikehadlow on Twitter for news and updates.

  • Select the tonic by clicking on a note button. The current tonic is highlighted in yellow.
  • Select a mode by clicking on a mode button.
  • Click on the outer 'chord' ring of either the chromatic note circle or the circle of fifths to show the
    triad associated with that note. Click on the chord ring again to cancel the chord display.
  • Chord ring colours:
    • Green: major chord.
    • Blue: minor chord.
    • Red: diminished chord.
  • Click on the interval ring, or the fretboard to toggle notes on and off.
  • Click the 'Tunings' menu to choose alternate tunings.
  • Click the 'Settings' menu to select a left handed fretboard and choose whether the
    fretboard shows note names or intervals.

By Mike Hadlow. Open source on Github.
